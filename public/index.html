Connect with your audience, anywhere

Oahu creates long term, meaningful relationships between you and your community

Oahu is an integrated marketing platform that enables you to build richer, more engaging campaigns.

Product

Optimized for engagement

Our suite of apps and libraries lets content producers redefine the rules of marketing, by reducing time to market, ensuring highly integrated social components, and optimizing campaigns for engagement.

Spend less time setting up all the mundane steps of your campaign, and focus on creating value instead.

Simple to use

Publish our pre-built apps to instantly offer your fans Quizzes, Instant Wins, branded pages and more. You can build entire campaigns without a single line of code.

Oahu is built to grow with your campaign.
We make it easy to publish and engage with your earliest content, and we follow you until you're launching your big bad main event.

Full service

We handle all the media, games mechanics, and user generated contents that make your campaigns richer and more compelling.

Completely managed assets means your videos are encoded with the highest quality, your shares and tracking are correctly set up, and your deployment is quick and painless.

Rich & Realtime

Leverage a robust set of rich realtime push components to create even more ambitious campaigns at a fraction of the time and cost.

Your users are notified instantly when they win a badge, a goodie, when a friend offers them something, and just about anything you can think of.

Insightful

With integrated metrics, Oahu shows you a precise breakdown of how your campaign’s doing, who’s playing, who’s sharing and much, much more.

Define custom funnels of engagement to get detailed metrics on your users flow, what's making them share, what's engaging them.

Cross campaign

Most importantly, We do all of this and more with an easy to use single sign-on, that authenticates your fans on all your platforms, enriches their profile across campaigns, allowing you to offer them precisely what makes them tick.

The more you use Oahu, the more value your audience gains from it. Reward them for their engagement at a global scale, and make them ask for more !
